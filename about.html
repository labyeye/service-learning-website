Welcome to our purpose of making the world brighter, together

Ananda Marga Children Home Bengaluru inspired by the great ideals of Spiritual Master Shri Shri Anandamurti ji towards upliftment of the Orphan, Poor and Neglected Children of Society, we started a Children Home (we do not call it an Orphanage because each one is the blessed child of Paramapursha) during the year 2004 at Bengaluru. There are 30 boys at present (most of them are parent-less), providing them with all essential facilities such as Food, Clothing, Education, Medical Care and Shelter at this campus. We do not discriminate anybody on race, religion or caste lines, treating all equally with due love, affection and care, keeping their welfare as prime importance. We feel happy to share with you that One Boy started his education from our Children Home right from 1st standard has excelled in studies and now studying in BTech (Computer Science) at IIT Pondicherry Technological University.
